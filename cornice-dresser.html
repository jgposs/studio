Cornice Dresser
Cornice is a modern take on the clothing dresser. As fashion consumers have shifted from suits and ties to luxury t-shirts and hoodies, clothing storage has stayed the same. Where traditional dressers hide clothes in drawers, Cornice's open shelves let you display prized garments while keeping them organized. Its airy, sculptural frame works equally well in a bedroom, walk-in closet, or retail environment.
