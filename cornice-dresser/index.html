Cornice Dresser
Cornice is a modern take on the clothing dresser. Fashion consumers have replaced suits and ties with t-shirts and hoodies, but clothing storage options have remained the same. Where traditional dressers hide clothes inside drawers, Cornice's open shelves display prized garments and provide streamlined storage. Airy, sculptural frame with numerous slim shelves to encourage single-layer garment organization. Designed for both home and retail use.

A'Design Iron Award Winner.
