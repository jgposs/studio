Boot Bag — Coyote
Ski boot bag with transformational design (U.S. Patent No. 10,376,028). Built to carry essential snow sports gear in style. Converts seamlessly from backpack to tote, providing alternative way to carry and fit into narrow spaces at home or in transit.

Reversible buckle engineered by Fidlock in Germany. Two interior mesh pouches fit up to 13" laptop/tablet. Grommets on boot pockets for drainage and circulation. Bungee straps to hold soft goods. D-rings provide attachment options for water bottle and more. Valuables pocket and key hook. 500D USA Nylon Cordura. Bark-tanned Wyoming elk leather trim. 50L capacity.
